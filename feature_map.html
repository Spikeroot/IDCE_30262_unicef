Improving global child health is a major goal of UNICEF. UNICEF has a wide range of health care measures around the world, and has established strong partnerships with governments and non-governmental organizations in various countries and associations. Understanding the local medical and health situation is very important for UNICEF.

Use the slider to switch data view below.

The map on the left shows the location and types of sanitation facilities, The types of health facilities are mainly hospitals, pharmacies, clinics, doctors and dentists.

The Heat Map on the right shows the distribution density of Health facilities. Red areas indicate high-density areas. Health facilities are mainly concentrated in the midwestern and southern coastal areas of Kenya.
